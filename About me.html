BARBARA MALJA

I am a designer who simultaneously studies industrial and graphic design, about to graduate in both careers. My passion lies in applying design to solve social problems, creating products and solutions that are aesthetically attractive and functional at the same time. I stand out for detecting latent needs and developing innovative methods that have a positive impact both in the industrial field and in the daily lives of users. My goal is to continue developing designs that facilitate and make more efficient both industrial and everyday processes.
